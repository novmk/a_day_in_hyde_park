I entered
    through

           Black Lion Gate.


   Usually,
      I come from the Marble Arch direction, but today I wanted to enter through
        a different gate for some reason.

                      next ∇






As I went through the gate,

           a long
           and wide path
           stretched out before me.



    I thought that since the gate was named Black Lion, there would be a statue of a black lion or something, but there wasn't.   next ∇



    Instead,

         there were tall trees lined up on both sides of the long road, providing an expansive and refreshing view. It was a feeling of the world being split into two parts. Above, there were white sky filled with greyish clouds,

The clouds were layered in a hazy blue-grey hue, reminiscent of elongated potato Gnocchi.

     and below, the ground was covered with green grass.  next ∇




The irregular horizon formed by

              the trees between

          the sky

            and the grass.



Beyond the layered clouds,
    I glimpsed the blue sky in the distance.



        The park was so vast that the weather seemed to vary in different areas.


To find a spot with better weather,


        I walked towards

          the direction of the blue sky.
